Who We Are

Let's Share, Let's Care

"No one should sleep hungry."

Save 2 Serve was born from a simple yet powerful vision: that the surplus food we often overlook can be the lifeline for someone else. We are a community-driven platform connecting generosity with necessity.


Our Impact Journey

By rescuing food from restaurants, hotels, and households, we ensure that every kilogram of surplus is redirected to provide nutrition and dignity to those who need it most.

Our Legacy Timeline

2020: The Spark

Save 2 Serve was founded with a small group of volunteers, believing that small change could make a big difference.

2022: Expanding Horizons

Reached our first 1,000 active savers and launched our first international food relief program.

2024: Technology Integration

Introduced the smart tracking dashboard, allowing users to transparently monitor their impact in real-time.

2026: Global Movement

Surpassed 10,000 active savers globally, fundamentally changing how communities approach micro-donations.

How It Works

1. Rescue

Identify surplus food in your home or business. Log your rescue efforts on your personal dashboard.

2. Donate

Schedule a drop-off or pickup for your surplus food through our simple donation system.

3. Serve

Watch as your rescued food reaches the tables of those in need — children, youngsters, and elders — creating real, visible impact in slum communities.

Types of Food Support

Cooked Meals

Providing freshly prepared, nutritious meals to individuals and families facing immediate hunger.

Dry Rations

Essential staples like rice, grains, and lentils that provide long-term food security for households.

Fresh Produce

Distributing fruits and vegetables to ensure that those in need have access to vital vitamins and nutrients.

Care Packages

Specially curated boxes containing a mix of food and essential hygiene items for the most vulnerable.

Donation Sectors

Restaurants & Hotels

Hotels and eateries donate surplus high-quality cooked meals to prevent food waste and feed the hungry.

Retail & Supermarkets

Supermarkets provide essential grocery staples and packaged goods that are near their sell-by dates.

Farms & Agriculture

Farmers contribute fresh seasonal harvests, ensuring nutritious produce reaches those in food-insecure areas.

Local Communities

Individual households and local groups pool their small savings to sponsor large-scale meal distributions.

A Message from our Founder

"I started Save 2 Serve with a single dollar and a vision. I saw how much food was wasted while families in my own neighborhood went hungry. By pooling our micro-savings, we've managed to provide over 100,000 meals this year alone. This isn't just my success story; it's ours."

Archana Sree

Founder & Visionary of Save 2 Serve
